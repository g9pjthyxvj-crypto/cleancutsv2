OUR
STORY.

BUILT FOR THE
CULTURE.

Clean Cuts was founded in 2018 on one belief — that a great barbershop is more than just a haircut. It's a place where you feel good from the moment you walk in to the moment you walk out.

We started with one chair and one barber in South LA. Today we're three master barbers deep, thousands of clients strong, and still delivering the same standard we set on day one — precision, care, and culture.

We don't rush. We don't cut corners. We listen to what you want and we deliver it every single time. That's the Clean Cuts promise and it's why our clients don't go anywhere else.
